The case for AGI

This prediction is not solely based on my personal conjectures but is supported by the consensus among leading experts in the AI field. These specialists, whose expertise is directly aligned with AI development, anticipate the realization of AGI—or something remarkably close to it—sooner rather than later, possibly within the next ten years.
